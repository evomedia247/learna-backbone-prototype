Qualifications

In order to make a decision on your application we need to be aware of all previous academic study, regardless of whether or not it is relevant to this application.

You must submit copies of degree transcripts/certificates (translated into English by a verifiable source where necessary) to authenticate the qualifications stated on the application form (this can be done by uploading documents). Your application cannot be considered by the University until these documents have been received.
