Personal Statement

All applicants must submit a personal statement to support their application, which must be between 500 and 3000 characters.

The personal statement is your opportunity to tell us:

  • Why you wish to follow your proposed programme?
  • What you hope to gain from completing this programme?
  • What you want to do after you complete your studies?

You should provide any information that would support your application for admission, such as relevant skills, experience and career goals.
